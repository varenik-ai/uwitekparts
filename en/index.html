Product Range

Covering Every Key System

We manufacture and supply spare parts for all major systems of a crawler bulldozer. Individually matched to the model, serial number and operating conditions.

⛓️

Undercarriage

Track chains, track shoes, carrier and support rollers, idlers, sprockets, and tensioners. For all Komatsu, Caterpillar and Liebherr series.

🛠️

Hydraulic System

Blade and ripper hydraulic cylinders, hydraulic pumps, control valves, high-pressure hoses, fittings, control valve blocks and seals for Komatsu D155, D275, D375, Caterpillar D8, D9, D10 and Liebherr PR764. Matched by machine serial number, shipped from Ankara worldwide.

⚙️

Engine

Piston kits, turbochargers, head gaskets, main and rod bearings, fuel injection pumps, injectors, radiators and cooling systems for Komatsu SA6D140, SA6D170, Caterpillar 3306, 3406, 3456, C15 and C18 engines. Every part matched to the specific series and year.

🔩

Transmission

Transmissions, clutch packs, disc brakes, final drives, final gear reducers and drive shafts for Komatsu D65, D155, D275, D355, D375, D475 and Caterpillar D6, D8, D9, D10. Buy a final drive or final gear reducer with direct shipment from our factory in Turkey.

🧰

Filters & Consumables

Oil, fuel, air and hydraulic filters, drive belts, seals and gaskets for Komatsu, Caterpillar and Liebherr bulldozers. Consumables matched to the exact model — from a D155 engine filter to a Liebherr PR764 hydraulic filter.

🧲

Seals

Rubber and metal gaskets, oil seals, O-rings and face seals for hydraulic cylinders, pumps and gearboxes of Komatsu, Caterpillar D6–D10 and Liebherr PR754 bulldozers. Seal repair kits made to order by part number or serial number.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a part by model?

Send us the brand, model and serial number of your bulldozer. We'll confirm compatibility and suggest matching parts within 24 hours.

Do you accept requests via WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp +90 536 889 27 80 is our main channel for quick requests. We also work by email at export@uwitekparts.com.

Do you need the bulldozer's serial number?

It's preferable — it lets us pinpoint the exact configuration and avoid matching errors. If you don't have it, we can work from the model and year of manufacture.

Can I order several systems at once?

Yes. We accept both single-item requests and combined orders covering hydraulics, engine, transmission and filters.

Which Komatsu and Caterpillar engines do you support?

We supply parts for Komatsu SA6D140, SA6D170, SA12V140 and Caterpillar 3306, 3406, 3456, C15, C18 engines. Let us know your engine model when requesting — we'll match the exact parts for your machine.

How is delivery from Turkey organized?

We ship from our factory in Ankara and arrange delivery to your warehouse worldwide with a full set of customs documents. Timelines and the logistics scheme are agreed when confirming the order.

Do you supply parts for Liebherr PR754 and PR764?

Yes. For Liebherr PR754 and PR764 bulldozers we manufacture and supply hydraulic components, engine and transmission parts, filters and seals. Requests are matched by the machine's serial number.

Is there a minimum order amount?

We work with both large batches and small single-item requests. There is no minimum order — we focus on the actual needs of your business.
